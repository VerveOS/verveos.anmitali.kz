About the project

VerveOS is an advanced mobile operating system developed on the basis of Android 15. We have created a system that combines the best practices of mobile development and innovative solutions. Our OS offers advanced customization and optimization capabilities, ensuring maximum performance and usability.

Key benefits

  • Modern version based on Android 15 with optimized performance.
  • Carefully selected pre-installed apps for maximum convenience
  • NikGapps integration for enhanced functionality
  • Built-in root - access for full control over the device
  • Open source for transparency and modifiability
  • Free distribution for accessibility to all users
  • Regular updates and community support
